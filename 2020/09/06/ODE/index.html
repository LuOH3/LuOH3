$\exists \lbrace t_m\rbrace uparrow\infty$ s.t. $\frac{-|\nabla F(\phi(t_m,\Gamma(\zeta)))|^3}{1+|\nabla F(\phi(t_m,\Gamma(\zeta)))|^2}$, thus $|\nabla F(\phi(t_m,\Gamma(\zeta)))|,\to 0$

note that $c<F(\phi(t_m,\Gamma(\zeta)))<F(\phi(0,\Gamma(\zeta)))$ be bounded

thus, $\lbrace \phi(t_m,\Gamma(\zeta))\rbrace$ is a Palais-Smale sequence

by Palais-Smale condition, $\phi(t_m,\Gamma(\zeta))\to x_c$, then we have $\nabla F(x_c)=0,F(x_c)>\alpha$
